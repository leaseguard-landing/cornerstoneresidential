Strategic Analysis for Cornerstone Residential

Consolidating LeasePro, Verification, and Monitoring Under One Provider

Cornerstone is already operating at scale across more than 200 communities and 20,000+ units. The opportunity here is not to introduce a new concept. It is to simplify the operating model, preserve the lapsed coverage functionality they value today, and evaluate whether LeaseGuard can deliver stronger economics and a cleaner platform experience.

Cornerstone currently works with multiple providers, including Foxen, Asset Protect, and Risk Release. This analysis focuses specifically on a LeasePro structure with verification and monitoring designed to support a consolidated approach while maintaining 100% compliance through a lapsed coverage endorsement.
